Our Story

Italian roots, New York rhythm.

Restro NYC is a modern Italian-American fusion restaurant built around handmade pastas, bright market produce, crisp pies, and familiar comforts served with quiet elegance. Our menu celebrates the charm of old-world cooking while keeping the energy, generosity, and polish of New York dining.

"A memorable meal should feel effortless: honest ingredients, graceful technique, and a table that invites you to stay."
- Chef Alessio Marino
